Buffed Pokemon:
---

---

Buffed Moves
---

Elemental Punches now have a 15% chance to apply status (up from 10%)  
Flamethrower, Ice Beam, and Thunderbolt now have a 20% chance to apply status (up from 10%)  
Tri Attack now has 85 base power (up from 80%), 20 power points (up from 10), and a 25% chance to apply status (up from 20%)  
Fire Blast, Blizzard, and Thunder now have a 30% to apply status (up from 10%)  
Magnitude now has a 1/255 chance to hit "Magnitude 11" for 250 base power  
Hyper Beam and Giga Impact no longer have a recharge turn if they get a KO  
Rock Throw, Rock Slide, Hyper Fang, and Play Rough are now 100% accurate (up from 90%)  
Hypnosis is now 65% accurate (up from 60%)  
Quick Attack now has 50 base power to make up for lack of type effectiveness  
Super Fang is now 95% accurate (up from 90%)  
Iron Head now has a 30% flinch chance (up from 20%)  
Hyper Voice is now a flinch move, with 15% chance to activate  
Will-o-Wisp now does damage with 30 base power  
Metronome now has 30 base power points (up from 10 in faithful/20 in polished)  
Moonblast now has a 30% chance to drop Sp.Atk (up from 10%)  
Swift now has 70 base power to make up for lack of type effectiveness  
Disarming Voice now has 60 base power to bring it in line with Feint Attack and Aerial Ace  
Ancientpower now has 10 power points and a 15% chance to activate  
Splash may or may not be buffed

Other Changes
---

Disabled RTC as the game runs on MBC5 which has no support (will re-enable eventually when PC adds MBC30 support)  
Bug-Catching Contest now has more variation in the Bug-Types available  

Added more variation to wild encounter tables (expect to find more regional forms, more rare Pokémon such as starters and eeveelutions, as well as 'adjacent-route' encounters such as Larvitar/Skarmory available pre-Falkner)
